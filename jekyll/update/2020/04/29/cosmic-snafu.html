Cosmic S.N.A.F.U.

Apr 29, 2020

In Cosmic S.N.A.F.U., you must survive for as long as possible as your ship moves uncontrollably through an asteroid field. Your ship’s power must be maintained to keep your shields and weapons online, and you must keep the radio in-tune to keep your radar working. And woe betide if your partner thinks you’re ignoring them…

This game was made during the 2020 Gamemaker’s Toolkist Game Jam. The music system consists of a main loop, the bass and drums with a melody that plays while you’re in the cockpit view. Whenevever you open one of your screens, a new melody will play and a lowpass filter is added to the main loop. Additionally, the music as a whole speeds up as things get more intense.

You can play the game at https://johngabrieluk.itch.io/cosmic-snafu
